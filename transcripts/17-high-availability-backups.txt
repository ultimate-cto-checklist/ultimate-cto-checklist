Voice Note #17 - High Availability & Backups
Date: 2026-02-02

---

The production database whenever there's serious money involved in a project needs to move to high availability.
Production server as well needs to have moved to at least two data center regions and not be tied to one place
or at least have a way to immediately promote another place so that the server never goes down.
The database needs to always get backed up and moved away from whatever plant provider is used.
Ideally, this is some backup as we window that makes sense or that allows it.
Or a read letter replica that's elsewhere as well, but this should not cost a lot of money to do.
It should be just either read replica or SKL databases that could be easily SKL files.
We should have point in time recovery for production as well.
