Voice Note #14 - Soft Deletes & Data Retention
Date: 2026-02-02

---

Any data that's critical in your database should never be
deletable. It should be soft deletables,
where your query is just ignored the data,
but the data is always there.
And then you should have periodic reviews of the data that's
deleted a long time ago and actually it's been
it's project and deleted unless for legal reasons you have to keep it.
